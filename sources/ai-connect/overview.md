# AI-Connect

## Definition
AI-Connect is the communication, routing, notification, and permission layer of the Applied Intelligence ecosystem.

## Status
PLANNED_INCOMPLETE

## Current State
AI-Connect is being added to the ecosystem structure as a placeholder source set. It is not complete and should not yet be treated as an active production agent.

## Ecosystem Role
AI-Connect connects agents, departments, workflows, reports, alerts, meetings, and human users so information moves to the right place at the right time with the right access.

## Boundary
AI-Connect does not replace AI-CIS, AI-Case, AI-ROI, AI-Quote, AI-RMA, or AI-Trace.
It connects them.

## Core Principle
One ecosystem. Connected flow. Clear communication.
